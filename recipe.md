Method for PANEER POPCORNS

1 STEP : Add rice flour, maida, corn flour and water, only to make a thick paste.
2 STEP : Then add ginger-garlic paste, chilli flakes, chilli powder, turmeric, and all other ingredients
mentioned under "spices". Add lemon juice and mix, make sure it is not to thin or flowy.
3 STEP : Now finally add paneer cubes and leave it for 10-15 mins.
4 STEP : After 15 mins take one paneer cube, roll or coat it in the bread crumbes and deep fry it.
Do this for every cube.
Plate the cubes nicely and now you can enjoy Paneer popcorns with your favrouite sauces.
